How does it work?

We offer a variety of billing options, including monthly and annual subscription plans, as well as pay-as-you-go pricing for certain services. Payment is typically made through a credit card or other secure online payment method. Just go on Gofunds, register and begin to scale.

Can I get a refund for my subscription?

We offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most of its subscription plans. If you are not satisfied with your subscription within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund. Refunds for subscriptions that have been active for longer than 30 days may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, you can log in to your account and navigate to the subscription management page. From there, you should be able to cancel your subscription and stop future billing.

Documentation is available on the company's website and can be accessed by logging in to your account. The documentation provides detailed information on how to use the app, as well as other resources.
